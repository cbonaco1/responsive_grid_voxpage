Why do we care about Mars?

Mars is the fourth planet out from the sun in our solar system, and earthlings have been dreaming about going there for a very long time.

As far back as War of the Worlds 1897, sci-fi writers have fantasized that Mars was home to intelligent extraterrestrial life, partly because of the planets striking color, proximity, and visible polar ice caps.
